Quality Ingredients

We pride ourselves on choosing the finest fresh, organic ingredients for all of our products. We insist on excellent quality and fantastic flavour.

Ethically Sourced

Whenever possible, our ingredients are locally sourced, when we need to look further we make sure to support fairtrade businesses.

Recycling

All of our drink and food packaging is fully recyclable. Also, feel free to bring your own containers for us to fill for you. We offer a 10% discount if you provide your own packaging.

Giving Back

We believe in supporting the community that supports us. 2% of our profits each year are donated to local charities which support community initiatives and amenities for everyone, young and old.
